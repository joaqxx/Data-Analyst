Coffee Chain Revenue Optimization and Product Performance Tracker

Developed an end-to-end Excel BI solution transforming fragmented transactional records into actionable insights on revenue trends and product performance. The dashboard identifies key revenue drivers and high-yield product categories to guide inventory and retention strategies.

Business Scenario

The coffee chain's managers had a lot of sales data, but they couldn't find simple answers. The key information was stuck in three separate files, making it hard to see the big picture. Managers had to spend too much time manually checking data just to answer basic questions about sales growth or their best customers. They needed one clear, automatic tool to quickly check how the business was doing.

Project Scope

The project required building a scalable data architecture to handle data from 2019 to 2022. Key deliverables included

  • Merged three distinct tables into a master dataset to consolidate fragmented records.
  • Created a user-friendly interface with dynamic filtering using Slicers and Timelines for granular analysis.
  • The report was changed to focus on finding the most important customers rather than just showing sales by country. This strategic focus helps the company plan its new customer rewards program.

Methodology & Technical Implementation

Data Cleaning and Preparation

  • Utilized Excel's built-in Check and duplicate removal features to ensure initial data integrity and clean the three source tables (orders, customers, products).
  • Applied consistent formatting to date and currency fields to prevent calculation errors and ensure visual consistency across the dashboard
  • The initial three source tables were cleaned and prepared before being joined, establishing a clean foundation for the subsequent data modeling step.

Data Modeling

  • Successfully utilized XLOOKUP and INDEX MATCH formulas to link the three initial source tables (orders, customers, and products) into a single Master Data Table.
  • This modeled data serves as the single source of truth and the foundation for all subsequent Pivot Tables and dynamic visualizations.
  • Developed custom calculated fields for Sales Revenue using arithmetic logic to power all downstream financial KPIs
  • Applied Multiple IF logic for specific data categorization (for example, confirming Loyalty Card status or grouping product attributes), enabling granular analysis.

Arabica is the clear cash cow, accounting for over 35% of all sales, confirming where inventory and marketing resources should be allocated.

Recommendations
  • Implement a high-tier loyalty program targeting the Top 5 Customers (and others in that tier) to ensure retention and capitalize on high sales volume.
  • Shift procurement budgets to secure Arabica supply chains to prevent stockouts on the highest revenue product
  • Review the performance of low-volume bean types for potential discontinuation to free up shelf space and capital
